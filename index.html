Welcome To Troop 442!

A Message from the Scoutmaster

Welcome to Troop 442 in Wylie, Texas! I’m Scoutmaster Sean Reynolds, and our Scout-led troop of +80 members is all about adventure and leadership. We stay active with weekly meetings, monthly campouts, and annual high-adventure trips, all planned by the Scouts themselves during our semi-annual "TOW" sessions. If your child is ready to master the outdoors—from hiking to canoeing—while learning vital leadership and service skills, we invite you to visit us and see what makes our program so rewarding.
