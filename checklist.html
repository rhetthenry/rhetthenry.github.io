Camping Essentials

This Checklist is to provide New scouts with the knowledge

to be able to pack their own bag!

Day Bag

  • Water Bottle
  • Flashlight
  • Extra Socks (in day bag)
  • Rain Coat
  • Sun Protection
  • Pocket knife (only if reqirements are met)
  • Trail Food
  • Bug Spray
  • First Aid Kit
  • SOS whistle

  • Night Bag Items

  • Extra Socks/Under Garments
  • Cloths for The Campout
  • Pajamas for campout
  • Toiletires
  • Sleeping Bag
  • Sleeping Pad
  • Tent and Tent Tarp
  • Any other Personal Items
